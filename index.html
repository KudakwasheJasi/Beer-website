India Pale Ale

India Pale Ale, or IPA, is a hoppy beer style known for its bold, bitter flavor. Our IPA offers a refreshing and aromatic blend of citrus and pine notes. Perfect for those who love a hop-forward beer with a crisp finish.

ABV

Alcohol by Volume

6.2 %

Our IPA has an ABV of 6.2%, making it a strong, yet balanced beer for all hop enthusiasts.

IBU

International Bitterness Units

51 .1

With an IBU of 51.1, this IPA strikes a perfect balance between bitterness and malt sweetness, giving you that classic IPA experience.

STYLE

The India Pale Ale style originated in England but is now most popular in the United States. Known for its hop-forward flavors and aromas, it remains one of the most beloved beer styles in the world.

Our IPA is brewed with a variety of hops that give it a punch of citrus and pine flavors, making each sip a refreshing experience for craft beer lovers.
